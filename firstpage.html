About Me

I am a 5 year old Brussels Griffon from Hermeston Oregon. My family adopted me in March 2012. We lived in San Francisco, California for a few years and I got to meet a lot of dogs. There was this one time I escaped from my dog walker and ran the city for one night. My family was so worried, but this man found me and took me in for the evening. I ran away to look for my family who was out of town. After that adventure, I get to fly on airplanes for family vacations. I love it!

In 2012 I met my best friend Alfonso Bendat at Miscone Park in SF. His family is really nice, and gets me. I spent a lot of time at their house chasing Alfonso and lounging on their many couches. Alfonso and I had a competition to see who could hide the pee spots on the rug. I won most of the time. #Winning!

In 2014, my family moved away from the city to a town 9 hours away by car, called Solana Beach, California. Now I don't get too see Alfonso much anymore. I miss him and his beard. AND I also miss his family who cared for me when mom and dad were busy.

The highlight of my life has been my family. They love and care for me so much, but my mom is a bit of a smotherer. My dad ignores me most of the time, and somehow we find a happy medium. Sometimes I like to sit between then to interrupt their conversations. They get thrown off so much!

The biggest change this year has been my dog walkers. They are all crazy, and only a handful of them actually do what my mom asks. My favorite dog walker is Rachael. She is sweet. Plus, she lives in Del Mar, and has an ocean view. Sick!

Debbie Espino 2015
